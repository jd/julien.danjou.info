--- title: Google Contacts for Emacs created: !!timestamp '2011-09-26 13:01:00' tags: - googlecontacts - emacs --- {% block excerpt %} {% mark excerpt %} I finally finished a thing I was really missing: accessing my Google Contacts from Emacs. {% endmark %} {% endblock %} That's now possible, thanks to my new [google-contacts.el]({{site.url}}/software/google-contacts.el) package. It includes searching for any contact and displaying the result in a window. You can also jump to a contact from [Gnus](http://gnus.org) by pressing a key, and complete e-mail addresses while composing a mail.
